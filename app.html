About

Hey,

Thanks for using BlurtItApp. My name is David and I built this as a little side project years ago to learn about building progressive web apps.

This new version before you is still very much built around the same premise - sharing full-screen messages quickly and discretely.

In this version, I've simplified the original codebase to reflect my learning, and I hope to add more features and design improvements over time.

I hope this little tool proves useful in your life as it has mine.

Blurt Responsibly!

How it works

Use BlurtItApp to share quick messages with someone nearby in three easy steps:

  1. Write your message
  2. Click BLURT IT!
  3. Share your message

Here are some scenarios where you might find this useful:

  • Placing an order at a loud bar.
  • Flirting with someone from afar.
  • Quietly robbing a bank.*
  • Sharing gossip with a friend.
  • Helping someone while at a stop light.
* I don't recommend this one.
